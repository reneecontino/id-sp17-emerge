about emerge

Boston. A city filled with hidden gems and a plethora of talent. With so much character and community, its’ qualities set it apart from any other city in America. There is so much knowledge and creativity floating inside the depths of this city, which is why it is considered the hub of the universe.

What really sets Boston apart from anywhere else is its students; eager to learn and striving for success. Emerge is a magazine brand that dedicates its’ purpose to showcasing young Boston talent in a variety of fields. Complete with interviews, articles, and photography, you do not want to miss out on discovering this months emerging artist.
